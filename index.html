Poet.css

Poet is a CSS typography library for responsive + eloquent websites. It is great for all sites looking for responsive and clean typography and great for blogs, news sites, and online books looking to make readible content. Poet offers base style improvments, as well as countless utilities to make your website sharper than ever!

Poet is great as a standalone library or to use along your favorite framework like Bootstrap. In fact a bunch of concepts in Poet come from Boostrap, however Poet expands on them by adding responsive utilities for elements such as headers to expand and shrink them based on device size. Poet makes for clean, crisp text on all devices, as well as uses concepts like The Golden Ratio to make text feel clean and readable.
